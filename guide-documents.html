Required Documents

Based on your proposed development, the following documents are generally required. All the documents should be in scanned PDF form.

  • E-Application
  • Payment Slip
  • Drawings of the Proposed Building
  • Certified Copy of Survey Plan
  • Ownership Documents (Deed / Lease agreement)
  • Building plans signed by a qualified architect / draughtsman
  • Structural drawings and certificate (for multi-storey buildings)
  • Copy of National Identity Card/Passport
  • Business Registration Certificate
  • Payement Reciept of Rates for Relevant Year
  • Copy of PPC
  • Copy of Approved Plan(if there any existing building)
  • If the Applicant is not the Owner of the Land, a Power of Attorney Allowing Applicant to Act on Behalf of the Owner

    • If the Landuse is Non-Residential or Apartment, Should Include Below Documents.

  • Fire Certificate
  • Clearance from CEB
  • Clearance from National Water Supply & Drainage Board
  • Clearance from Condominium Authority(if it is a Condominium Property)
